About JKICT

We aim to make a brighter and happier future.

We are proud and honored to be a part of the solution helping others to stay healthy and safe. JKICT specializes in the sourcing and distribution of innovative medical supplies, devices, and leading medical technology for military, governmental, hospital, and commerical markets.


To bring you the best and most innovative technology, JKICT provides advanced high technology medical equipment through its partnership with the Korean Medical Devices Industrial Cooperative Association (KMDiCA), with over 500 members of medical equipment manufacturers. Leveraging our years of experience and relationships in the Korean medical manufacturer marketplace, JKICT selects medical manufacturers that produce high quality medical products and devices to meet the needs of our distributors and customers.
