McKesson Autoclave Pouches safegaurd the sterilization process of surgical instruments. Our disposable, self-sealing pouches are easy to open, with internal and external indicators, eliminating the need for autoclave tape and assuring proper exposure time, high temperatures and saturating steam presence. Available in a variety of sizes for use with both steam and gas sterilization processes.
